Conversations

This collection consists of conversations overheard by me in New York City and some of the conversations I've had with my friends/people I've met in New York.

My friend Saul and I talk outside my building in Harlem. We talk about his family and discuss Paulo Coelho's books.

-December 2016

My friend Valentina and I chat at Jean-Michel Basquiat's grave at the Green-Wood cemetery in Brooklyn. Just moments before I started recording, we spoke to a family who had come to visit the artist's grave like us. Coincidently, they happened to know Alexis Adler who was Basquiat's roommate in the early 80s.
